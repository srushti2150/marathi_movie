Audio Languages : Marathi Genre : Drama, Action kagar is a 2015 Marathi superhero film starring Shreyas Talpade and Amruta Khanvilkar in lead roles. The film is based on the legend of Baji who takes it upon himself to protect the common people against oppression and injustice. Being a messiah for the people in his village Baji stops a wicked villager named Martand, who is greedy to find the treasure hidden under the village. Baji's sword, his bow-arrow and his white stallion are his trademark and define him as Baji.
